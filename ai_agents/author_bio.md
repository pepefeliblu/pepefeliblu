## Author Bio / Context

Juan Rueda is a **Senior Software Engineer** with a strong focus on **frontend architecture, web platforms, and developer experience**.

He has extensive experience building **production-grade web applications** using **TypeScript, Angular, JavaScript, and Node.js**, and has worked across the stack with technologies such as **NestJS, .NET, PostgreSQL, MongoDB, and MSSQL**. He has led and organized development efforts, acted as a technical lead, and collaborated closely with product managers, designers, and cross-functional teams.

Juan has worked on **enterprise and fintech platforms**, contributing to products that reached significant business milestones. He is experienced with **Agile/Scrum methodologies**, has mentored other developers (especially around security and best practices), and values clean architecture, maintainability, and long-term thinking over short-term hacks.

In addition to enterprise work, Juan has built **complex frontend systems**, including 3D-driven applications and product configurators, where performance, scalability, and user experience were critical. He enjoys exploring system design, cloud infrastructure, and tooling that improves developer productivity.
